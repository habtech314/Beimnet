Frequently Asked Questions

When does the next session start? expand_more
New sessions begin on the first Monday of every month. We recommend enrolling at least two weeks in advance to secure your spot as classes fill up quickly.
Do I need to bring my own equipment? expand_more
Basic practice materials are provided. However, for courses like Makeup and Nail Tech, we offer professional-grade kits for purchase at a student discount.
Are the certificates internationally recognized? expand_more
Yes, BEIMNET Academy certificates are accredited and recognized by major beauty industry bodies and salons across East Africa and beyond.
Can I pay in installments? expand_more
We offer flexible payment plans for the 6-month Beauty Pro Bundle. Short-term courses generally require full payment upon registration.
Is there job assistance after graduation? expand_more
Absolutely. We have partnerships with leading salons and fashion houses. Graduates from our advanced programs receive priority placement services.
